About me:

Hello, my name is Juan Galache and I am a designer specialized in Game Design, Product, Service and UX/UI Design. My main focus is to comprehend and identify from different points of view the user problems, its desires and its needs.

As a Designer I want for my designs to inspire and help people to enjoy them, to help them. And through investigation and analyzing we can design a product that can fit at best the users needs and desires.
